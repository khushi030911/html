Traditional Cha (Tea) Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk (or to taste)
  • 1 tsp black tea leaves (or 1 tea bag)
  • 1-2 tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1-2 cardamom pods (optional)
  • Fresh ginger slice (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add tea leaves, cardamom, and ginger if using.
  3. Let it simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add milk and sugar. Let it boil again.
  5. Strain the tea into a cup.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
